Visiting the RPCV Career Center in Washington, D.C.

The RPCV Career Center is located in Peace Corps Headquarters in Rm. 1200, just to the left of the lobby. If you would like to visit the Career Center, please note that upon arrival at headquarters, you will need to clear security and provide a government-issued photo ID. Once through security, you can proceed to the Career Center to the left of the lobby.

The hours and procedures for entering the RPCV Career Center are subject to change. Please visit the Peace Corps website before you visit for the most current information.
