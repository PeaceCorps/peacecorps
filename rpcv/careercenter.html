Information

The RPCV Career Center is located in Peace Corps Headquarters in Rm. 1200, just to the left of the lobby.

For RPCVs wishing to visit the new RPCV Career Center location, please follow the steps below to gain access:
  1. Come to the main entrance to headquarters, 1111 20th St., NW, Washington DC 20526.
  2. Clear security (similar to airport security).
  3. Report directly to the Career Center where you will need to sign in at the window.

The hours and procedures for entering the RPCV Career Center are subject to change. Please check this page before your visit for the most up-to-date information.
