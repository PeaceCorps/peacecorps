Medical Benefits

Back
  • During Service: All volunteers receive comprehensive medical and dental benefits during service. The Peace Corps covers all vaccination and anti-malarial prescriptions, which begin prior to departure. Each post has a Peace Corps medical Officer to provide primary care to volunteers. All volunteers receive full medical insurance during service; it covers 100% of primary care, hospitalization, medical evaluation, all prescriptions including birth control and dental care needs. Volunteers are covered by workman’s compensation for injuries incurred during their period of service.
  • After Service: Peace Corps Volunteers have access to affordable health insurance for up to 18 months following Volunteer service. The Peace Corps pays the first month’s premium and you then have the option to purchase a reasonably priced insurance policy to cover you and qualified dependents.
