Eligibility - Couples

Back

All applicants who are applying for Peace Corps service together must be married for at least a year by the time they begin service.

Married Couples

Applicants who are legally married must both apply and qualify for assignments going to the same in-country site. A marriage certificate must be provided prior to invitation.

Same-Sex Domestic Partners

Applicants in a same-sex domestic partnership must both apply and qualify for assignments going to the same in-country site. Same-sex couples must sign an affidavit before the couple departs for service that will serve as evidence of their domestic partnership.
