Back

An applicant for Peace Corps cannot be on parole or probation to any court during service. An applicant on probation cannot be nominated for service. This includes unsupervised probation, plea before judgment, deferred sentencing, deferred judgment or any other type of obligation to a court. Probation includes any legal judgment that dismisses a charge after a period of time during which the applicant fulfills legal obligations (fines, AA classes, community service, etc.)

An applicant with prior convictions/actions pending must have all issues completely satisfied before legal clearance can be granted during the application process. Each individual’s legal history will be reviewed in conjunction with the “whole applicant”.
