Third Goal Resources

  • Materials for Presentations to Community Groups

    Find Peace Corps resources to support your Third Goal activities including sample presentations, a media kit, downloadable coloring pages for kids, and much more. Also, contact us before your next event and receive Peace Corps promotional materials to share during your event!

  • Peace Corps Week Events

    Each year, Peace Corps organizes Peace Corps Week events to celebrate the creation of the Peace Corps. Take part in organized events - or come up with your own! - to support Peace Corps' Third Goal while remembering your Peace Corps experience.

  • Speakers Match Program

    Offered through the Paul D. Coverdell World Wise Schools program, the Speakers Match Program connects returned Volunteers with classrooms across the United States. Sign up today and Peace Corps will share your email address with interested schools and community groups in your area.

  • National Peace Corps Association

    Connect with the National Peace Corps Association, a privately-run non-profit organization, and other RPCV groups.
